We are foodpanda

foodpanda is one of the world’s largest online food ordering marketplaces. We are a highly motivated and talented team, driven by the ambition to become the global synonym for online food ordering.We offer exceptional opportunities for initiative, responsibility and leadership in a multinational environment with entrepreneurs from over 40 nationalities. We launched our first market, Singapore, in 2012 and are headquartered in Berlin. Currently we are active across Asia, Eastern Europe, Latin America, and the Middle East, employing more than 3,700 people worldwide.In June 2014 the foodpanda group won the European Tech Startup Award as best E-Commerce Start-Up. The company has raised over US $300 Million from various investors since its launch in 2012.
